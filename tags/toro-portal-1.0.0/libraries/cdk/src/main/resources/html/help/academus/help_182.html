Notification Outbox

Your notification Outbox allows you to view sent notifications, delete sent notifications and view reports on which individuals have read the notification.

To view sent notifications:

  1. Click the Outbox tab within the Notification portlet.
  2. Click the message subject to view the details of the messages and report on recipient read/unread status.
  3. Click Report to view a list of recipients and the read/unread status for each recipient.

 

 

Deleting Sent Notifications

To delete notifications from your outbox:

  1. Place a checkmark next to each notification you wish to delete.
  2. Click the Delete button.
  3. Verify that your selection(s) appear in the left navigation pane under the Messages You Have Selected to Delete heading.
  4. Click the Yes radio button to confirm the deletion of these messages.
  5. Click the Submit button.

The message will be removed from your outbox
