Editing Scores

After your enrolled users have submitted assignments and/or exams to you either online or through a traditional classroom setting, you'll want to record their scores. You can do that by editing the scores in the Gradebook channel.

  1. In the Gradebook channel's main view, locate the column for the scores that you would like to edit.
  2. Click the radio button beneath the column OR select the column from the drop-down menu at the bottom of the Gradebook channel. By selecting the column in either of these methods, the alternate method will automatically be selected as well.
  3. Once your column is selected, click the Column Scores link or the Edit icon next to it at the bottom of the Gradebook channel.
  4. You should now only see the column you have selected, with each user's scores in editable text boxes.
  5. Add or change the scores for any of the users that you deem necessary. You don't have to give each user a score at this time.
  6. When you are satisfied with the scores you have added or edited, click the Update button.
  7. You will now see the main Gradebook view, with updated scores for the column you selected.
